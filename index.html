BSCM President Message

BSCM returns this year in its 26th edition under the title PEOPLE 5.0: The Next Generation of Workforce. This topic has been carefully chosen to highlight the blend of human and technological elements, especially HI & AI.

Therefore, the Board of Directors and the organizing committee invite you to engage fully with international and regional experts.

We welcome your participation — sponsors, participants, or supporters in HR development — to enhance performance and productivity.

Don’t miss it! Join us at the congress.

Ahmed Al Zayani

ABOUT THE CONGRESS

PEOPLE 5.0 refers to the evolution of how humans and technology interact, especially in the workplace. It’s inspired by the concept of Society 5.0 (introduced in Japan), which envisions a super-smart society where technology like AI, IoT, and robotics is integrated into daily life to enhance human capabilities rather than replace them.
